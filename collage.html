Collage

The satisfying slice of scissors going through paper to create an image in my head, knowing that as you take the work from other sources, it will form itself into a new piece. Much of my work is very personal and autobiographical in nature. Chance elements, in conjunction with words, phrases and quotes direct the majority of my pieces, as inks, paper, collage elements and paint interact with each other on the flat surface. My 3 filing cabinets of images and clippings will all end up in my work, somehow in time.
